About Hammond PSD

Hammond Public Service District commits to improving and maintaining the public health protection and performance of our drinking‑water and distribution assets while minimizing long‑term operating costs. We invest in cost‑effective renewal and replacement, provide the highest‑quality customer service possible, and manage water resources to meet evolving regulations and customer needs.
